The Problem

Your AI agents are blind

They can't see your screen, hear your voice, or remember what you were doing five minutes ago. Every conversation starts from scratch. Every context switch means re-explaining everything.

Can't see your screen

Your agent has no idea what app you're in, what error is on screen, or what you're looking at right now.

Can't hear you

Voice context vanishes the moment you stop talking. Your agent can't reference what you said two minutes ago.

No memory

Every session resets. Your agent doesn't know what you were doing before, what you copied, or what you typed.

How It Works

Three modules. Total awareness.

ContextPulse runs as a single lightweight daemon in your system tray. It captures everything your AI agent needs to understand your working context.

Sight

Auto-captures your screen every 5 seconds with full OCR. Tracks clipboard changes, active windows, and app usage patterns.

  • Screen capture + OCR
  • Clipboard monitoring
  • Activity timeline
  • Rolling buffer (tunable)
10 MCP tools

Voice

Hold-to-dictate with local Whisper transcription. Self-improving vocabulary learns your jargon and auto-corrects misheard words.

  • Hotkey dictation (Ctrl+Space)
  • Local Whisper model
  • Auto-learning vocabulary
  • Transcription history
3 MCP tools

Touch

Monitors typing patterns and mouse activity. Detects when you correct voice dictation errors and feeds improvements back to Voice.

  • Typing burst detection
  • WPM + backspace ratio
  • Correction detection
  • Click, scroll, drag events
3 MCP tools
Integration

Speaks MCP natively

ContextPulse exposes 21 tools through the Model Context Protocol. No APIs to configure, no tokens to manage. Your AI agent calls ContextPulse tools just like any other MCP server.
